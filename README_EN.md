# Love and Hate

A small narrative-driven game focused on moral choices and the fate of humanity.


## 📖 Synopsis

The world has been divided into two powerful factions: **Alpha** and **Beta**.

Both sides have entered into conflict due to the growing scarcity of the resources required for survival.

The protagonist of the story is **Long**, heir to the leadership of the Alpha faction.  
He carries the responsibility of making decisions that will determine the future of his people.

On the opposing side stands **Lyana**, the heir to the power of the Beta faction.

Throughout the story, Long will face a constant internal struggle between two forces:

- **Love**, representing hope, empathy, and the survival of humanity.
- **Hate**, representing resentment, destruction, and the desire to inflict pain.

Each decision will shape the fate of the world.

## 🎮 Game Purpose

Players will make a series of choices based on **love or hate**.

Each decision influences the progression of the story and ultimately determines **the future of humanity**.

At the end of the game, players will receive one of several possible endings depending on the choices they made.
